THE PROJECT

PROMOTING INCLUSIVE AND SIGNIFICANT REMOTE TEACHING AND LEARNING IN THE EAST AFRICAN HIGHER EDUCATION

Objective 1

To establish the situation of the existing policies and regulations that support preparedness for remote teaching and learning in higher education in response to pandemics.

Objective 2

To appraise the emerging policy and regulatory best practices for supporting remote teaching and learning during pandemics..

Objective 3

To diagnose the gaps in existing policy and regulatory mechanisms by benchmarking with the best practices appraised in (ii).

Objective 4

To develop a multi-level decision support framework for remote teaching and learning during pandemic.

Objective 5

To develop a decision support system prototype for remote teaching and learning during pandemic.
